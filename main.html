Manali

My dream destination which has lot of feelings and happnies


  1. Solang Valley – Famous for paragliding and snow activities.
  2. Rohtang Pass – A scenic mountain pass with breathtaking views.
  3. Hadimba Temple – An ancient wooden temple surrounded by cedar forests.
  4. Old Manali – Known for its peaceful vibe and cozy cafes.
  5. Manu Temple – A sacred site dedicated to Sage Manu.

Must-Try Foods

  • Siddu – A traditional Himachali steamed bread.
  • Thukpa – A hot Tibetan noodle soup perfect for cold weather.
  • Momos – Steamed dumplings with delicious fillings.
  • Chana Madra – A yogurt-based chickpea curry.
  • Babru – A Himachali-style stuffed bread similar to kachori.
